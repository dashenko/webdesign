About “In and Out of Consciousness: Greatest Hits 1990–2010”

Robbie’s second greatest hits album, released October 11, 2010, celebrating 20 years of his career. The album features “Shame”, co-written with Gary Barlow, which is the first time since 1995 they had worked together.

Robbie said of the album:

It’s incredible to listen to the album and realise that it’s already been 20 years of making music and playing gigs. And the great thing about the album is that it’s not only a celebration of my past but also a bridge to the future. The fact that part of the future includes a name from my past makes it all the more poignant for me.
